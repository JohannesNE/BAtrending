# Cardiac output

Cardiac output by two methods, RV and IS, for 12 subjects

## Usage

``` r
CO
```

## Format

A data frame with 60 rows and 3 variables:

- sub:

  Subject

- rv:

  Cardiac output, radionuclide ventriculography (RV)

- ic:

  Cardiac output, impedance cardiography (IC)

## Source

<https://www-users.york.ac.uk/~mb55/datasets/datasets.htm>

## References

Bland JM, Altman DG. (1999) Measuring agreement in method comparison
studies. Statistical Methods in Medical Research 8, 135-160. (data
provided by Dr LS Bowling)
